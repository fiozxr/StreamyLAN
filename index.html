Capabilities

Everything You Need
to Stream Local

No cloud. No subscriptions. Just your device and your Wi-Fi.

High-Performance Server

Multi-threaded HTTP/1.1 socket server streams media to any device — Mac, PC, Linux, iOS, Android, Smart TVs — via clean browser-native HTML5 players.

Advanced Video Controls

Dynamic 0.5x–2.0x playback speed, smart resume with local storage, double-tap fullscreen, and full keyboard shortcut support.

Auto LAN Discovery

UDP subnet scan auto-detects other StreamyLAN nodes on your Wi-Fi. Tap Connect to browse peer libraries instantly.

Live Firewall

Real-time network monitor shows all active client IPs and user agents. Block intrusive clients or clear cache instantly.

CLI Terminal Friendly

Pre-generated, copyable cURL, wget, and VLC terminal commands for downloading or streaming over the local network.

HTTP Range Support

Standard Range: bytes chunked serving for instant, buffer-free timeline scrubbing in browsers and external players like VLC.

Workflow

Three Steps to
Your Private Cinema

1

Add Media

Launch StreamyLAN on your Android device. Tap Add Media to choose any video, audio, or document from storage.

2

Start Server

Select your TCP port and tap the central pulse button. Your local URL appears instantly — e.g., http://192.168.1.100:8080

3

Stream Anywhere

Open any browser on your TV, laptop, or tablet. Browse, download, or play videos with premium controls — zero buffering.
